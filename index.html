Talking pain: how to support your patients in pharmacy

Date : Now 23 - 2023
Time : 19:30 - 20:15
Speaker :Faheem Ahmed (M Pharm)

Join our Pain Management Webinar and Unlock the Power to Ease Discomfort

Pain is a complex condition which can only be completely understood by the person experiencing it. However, as the most accessible healthcare providers, community pharmacies can play a vital role in helping patients take action and assisting them in safe and effective management of acute and chronic pain.

Join us for a free 45-minute CPD accredited, Pain Management Webinar designed to empower you and your staff with effective strategies for understanding, managing and reducing pain in your patients’ daily life.

Agenda

Understanding Pain:

Exploring the fundamentals of different pain types.

Medication Management:

Find out the role of different pain relief medications.

Non-Pharmacological Pain Relief:

Learn about alternative pathways, such as heat and cold therapy, stretching exercises which could complement traditional treatment.

Managing Chronic Pain:

If you are dealing with sufferers of chronic pain, what are the long-term strategies that can help them cope with the pain and how these can improve their quality of life?

Q&A Session:

Please send your questions to our panel of experts either in advance of or during the webinar. We will endeavour to address your concerns individually.

Registration:

Reserve your spot today by visiting. Registration is free and attendees will receive CPD module completion certificate.

Who should attend:

All colleagues who deal with patients suffering from acute or chronic pain and work in a pharmacy setting community, practice, PCN, hospital and industry.
